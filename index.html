Guide

The Everest base camp trek is one of the most popular treks in Nepal. Apart from reaching the foot of glorious Mt Everest, you will have the opportunity to marvel at unforgettable views of the Solu Khumbu region. Also, this trek leads through Namche Bazar, a trading town between Tibet and the Sherpa, located at 3,440 m above sea level.
